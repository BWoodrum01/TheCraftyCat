About Us

We are a Cat Cafe located in Mewville, KY. Our mission is to provide a comfortable cafe experience, while also finding homes for as many cats as we can. So far, we have found homes for over 200 cats!

We offer a weekly menu of coffee, as well as a weekly "menu" of cats at our cafe.

(All of which will be open for adoption, so you can come and visit and get to know them!)
